What tigers do during the day

Tigers are a very wild animal. They hunt for many things big and small. when tigers get together they mate and then go seperate ways. A female tiger makes 2 to 3 cubs. The female tiger raises the cubs alone. After two years tigers gain independence. Some tigers that live in the wild rest more in the day and hunt at night.

Diet

Tigers are big animals that like to eat all sizes of animals, but thier favorite food are : deer, buffalo, bison, tapir, orangutan, monkeys, camels, fish, and wild boars. Even if those are their favorites they might not be around so they will eat dogs, crocodiles, leopards, and sloth bears.

How they hunt their food

Tigers get their food by trying to ambush their prey. They will quietly follow their prey then jump on them to capture it's food. But because some tigers too old to hunt or injured they tend to try and get humans. So beware if you can.
